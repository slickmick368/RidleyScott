G L A D I A T O R

The film Gladiator is set in Rome in 180 A.D., the dying Emperor Marcus Aurelius names his successor, Maximus Decimus Meridius. However, the emperors son, Commodus, murders his father and seizes the throne. Maximus is sentenced to slavery and his family are killed. He becomes a gladiator and rises through the ranks of the of the arena to avenge the deaths caused by Commodus.
