A L I E N

The film Alien is set in the distant future, where Nostromo - a spaceship owned by a corporation - returns to Earth after an expedition. On it’s ships journey home, the crew are woken from hyper sleep from a distress call from a distant planet. The crew follow the source of the distress call and subsequently end up exploring the planet, finding an abandoned spaceship containing thousands of eggs. When a crew member goes to take a closer look, a parasite leaps out and attaches to his face. He’s brought back onboard and they crew go back to earth. The crew member wakes up and is apparently fine, the parasite has died and everything goes back to normal. But not for long.
