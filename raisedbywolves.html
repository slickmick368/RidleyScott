R A I S E D  B Y  W O L V E S

When Earth is close to extinction and the human race is divided over religious differences, atheists and believers, an android architect creates two androids named Mother and Father. He gives these androids one mission - to start a peaceful, atheist colony on a new planet. Mother and Father must raise a number of human children alone on this new strange planet.
